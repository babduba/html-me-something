My current Audible tech books playlist

  • Scrum: The Art of Doing Twice the Work in Half the Time
  • The Phoenix Project
  • Beyond the Phoenix Project

Scrum

The Art of Doing Twice the Work in Half the Time

By: Jeff Sutherland, J.J. Sutherland

If you want a book about the benefits of using scrum without going into the details of how to scrum, this is the book for you. The author takes you through a journey of failed waterfall methodoligies and how scrum can overcome those shortfalls of the same. I really enjoyed listening to this book especially as our team transitioned to using scrum and I needed a solid introduction of scrum.

The Phoenix Project

A Novel about IT, DevOps, and Helping Your Business Win

By: Gene Kim, Kevin Behr, George Spafford

The Phoenix Project was very relatable to me coming from a System Administration background and having gone through multiple legacy system swaps and the chaos of go-lives. The book introduced agile concepts, use of Kanban boards, and other strategies to streamline processes in an IT departments by creating processes that identify types of work and complete them efficiently.

Beyond the Phoenix Project

The Origins and Evolution of DevOps

By: Gene Kim, John Willis

I did enjoy the Phoenix Project and when I saw Beyond the Phoenix Project, I couldn't resist but to listen to it. The format is a conversation that takes you through the journey of DevOps, and along the way, you pick up concepts and names of industry leaders who pioneered some of the ground work in DevOps. As you listen to this conversation, you will find yourself adding more books to read about some of the concepts. Great book if you want more knowledge into DevOps.
